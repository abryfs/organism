# North Star

## Who

Founder-engineers who build with Claude Code. They've installed GSD, Superpowers, gstack — maybe other tools too. Each tool is good at something. But Claude doesn't know how to use them together. It picks one, runs it sequentially, misses the strengths of the others. The founder ends up juggling 85+ commands across three systems, guessing which to invoke when.

## Pain

AI coding tools made building so fast that founders lost ownership. They can't explain what was built or why. Tests get skipped. Market grounding gets skipped. Features get built for nobody. The speed that was supposed to be an advantage becomes a liability — shipping fast in the wrong direction.

Meanwhile, each tool ecosystem solves part of this: GSD tracks state, Superpowers enforces TDD, gstack does browser QA. But they don't coordinate. Installing all three doesn't make them work together. Claude treats them as independent skill sets and picks whichever it encounters first.

## What Organism Does

The nervous system. Routes the right skill from the right system to the right moment. Enforces a coordination protocol where Gut checks alignment, Brain plans with market context, Spine gates quality, and Hands verifies in reality. The founder stays in control of the product while the organism coordinates the tools.

Anti-vibe-coding. Every feature traces to a real user. Every claim has evidence. Every shortcut gets flagged. The founder owns what they ship because the organism forced them to think before building.

## One Metric

Does the founder ship the right thing, with evidence, and can explain every decision? Not lines of code. Not speed. Ownership of the product.

## Anti-Goals

- Not a framework. No runtime dependencies, no build step, no lock-in.
- Not a replacement for GSD, Superpowers, or gstack. A coordinator.
- Not autonomous. The founder decides. The organism enforces discipline and surfaces information.
- Not another vibe coding accelerator. Slower where it matters (thinking), faster where it doesn't (execution).
- Not locked to three systems. Open to any repo that makes the workflow better.

## Current Focus

Close the enforcement gap. The coordination protocol exists but enforcement is shallow (6/10). The remaining work: E2E tests that prove agents follow the protocol, systems.json as runtime infrastructure not documentation, learning loop tooling, deeper hook integration. Get from 6/10 to 8/10.
